PROPOSAL

Security cameras are video cameras that are used to monitor space. They frequently have an IP network or recording device linked to them. With the rapid growth of technology, especially Artificial Intelligence, a number of new ways have been approached to present AI into the video surveillance system. Artificial Intelligence for video surveillance employs computer software programs that analyse audio and image data. However, is it really a good choice to rely on artificial intelligence that may be able to renovate the entire surveillance industry?

In choosing this topic, we are attempting to determine whether Artificial Intelligence is actually advantageous or disadvantageous to many of our security applications nowadays. We were particularly interested in seeing if it constitutes a security breach or is totally beneficial with unlimited functionality and close to 100% accuracy.
