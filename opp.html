JUSTIFICATION & OPPORTUNITIES

PRODUCTIVITY, EFFICIENCY & BUSINESS OPPORTUNITIES

AI security cameras provide a majority of answers to the clients security problems. Reviewing video footage to gather more information about events, may appear simple, but without AI video analytics, they can be extremely difficult and time-consuming. Security personnel working on large campuses and business premises such as airports, universities, supermarkets, and other similar locations would acknowledge this.

Even so, such locations have thousands of cameras installed, and each camera runs continuously, amassing massive amounts of video footage, making searching for this data time consuming and expensive. Nevertheless, it is critical for investigations and law enforcement officers, which is where AI video analytics can come in handy. Modern AI-powered video surveillance systems allow users to search for a specific action or object in all footage much faster than before.

Artificial intelligence in security cameras has begun to make an effect on human jobs. This trend will continue as these technologies become more advanced and developed. Employers no longer need to hire people who spend the majority of their time sitting in front of computer screens and staring at nothing, thanks to AI-based surveillance systems.
